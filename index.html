I am a PhD Research fellow at Distributed, Embedded and Intelligent Systems (DEIS) research group in the department of Computer Science at Aalborg University, Denmark. I received a Masters Degree in Computer Science from The University of Lahore (UOL), Pakistan in 2017. My research focuses on implementing formal methods and machine learning based tools for cost optimization of residential heating control solutions, energy-aware buildings, strategy synthesis, and multi-objective optimization. I have served as a Software Engineer at Goodlogics Pvt Ltd, Lahore, Pakistan. I have also served as a lecturer (Computer Science) at The University of Lahore, Pakistan.

Highlights

  • I am currently working on projects that aim to design intelligent heat-pump controllers.
  • My PhD is about “Strategy Synthesis Techniques for Multi-objective Optimization”, supervised by Prof. Kim Guldstrand Larsen, Prof. Jiří Srba and Assistant Prof. Peter Gjøl Jensen.
  • I am exploring various approaches to optimize energy cost and comfort, specifically in domestic heat-pump control systems. We incorporate electricity spot prices and weather data in our models that have been continuously tested and adjusted to the conditions in a virtual family home ( click here for detailed description).
